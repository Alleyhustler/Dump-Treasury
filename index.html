The Treasury Mechanism

1

Whale Exit Detected

A major holder exits their position. The blockchain registers the transaction as it propagates through the network, triggering the treasury monitoring protocol.

2

Treasury Captures Tax

A small, calculated tax is automatically extracted from the exit transaction. This fee is deposited directly into the Dump Treasury smart contract, ready for redistribution.

3

Proportional Rebate Distributed

All verified small holders—wallets under the defined threshold—automatically receive a proportional rebate. The distribution is instant, fair, and algorithmic.

4

Treasury Resets

The Dump Treasury completes the cycle and returns to monitoring state. The system stands ready, waiting for the next whale exit to trigger the next redistribution event.

How It Works

Dump Treasury monitors sell pressure across participating tokens.

When a major exit is detected, the treasury triggers redistribution.

Wallets under a defined threshold automatically receive a rebate.

No applications. No claims. Just redistribution.
